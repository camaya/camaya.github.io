Hacking cars in Latin America

2016 May 28

"Hackeando carros en Latinoamerica" is the title of a talk given by Jaime Restrepo (@DragonJAR). It's a fascinating talk about a lot of security flaws he found in Chevrolet vehicles. The funny thing is that everything started with a simple email, and from there everything escalated very quickly. It was really really bad.

Some weeks ago I was talking about this talk with a friend, a friend who knows very little Spanish and he said that he'd love to watch it. I told him that unfortunately as far as I knew it was only available in Spanish, but that I was going to ask if there were an English version or at least the Spanish version with English subtitles. And I did it:

I hope you enjoy it and learn as much as I did.
